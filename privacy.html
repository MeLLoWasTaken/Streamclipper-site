Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

StreamClipper is a personal, single-user tool operated by its owner to edit clips from the owner's own livestream recordings and publish them to the owner's own social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Rumble).

What data the tool accesses

The tool accesses only the account of the person who operates it, using credentials and access tokens that the operator generates for their own accounts. Through TikTok's Content Posting API it uploads video files and post metadata (titles, hashtags) to the operator's own TikTok account.

What data is collected from others

None. The tool has no users other than its operator, no visitors, no accounts, no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking. It does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information from any third party.

Data storage

Video files, transcripts, and access tokens are stored only on the operator's own computer and are never transmitted to anyone except the platforms the operator explicitly publishes to (e.g., TikTok, YouTube, Rumble) via their official APIs.

Data deletion

Because no third-party data is collected, there is nothing to delete. The operator can revoke the tool's access at any time from their TikTok, Google, or Rumble account security settings, and can delete all locally stored files directly.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the repository owner via the contact information on this site.
